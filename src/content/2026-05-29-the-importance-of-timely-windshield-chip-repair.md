# Introduction to Windshield Chip Repair
Windshield chips can be a frustrating and inevitable part of driving. Whether caused by a pebble on the road or a stray rock from a construction site, these small damages can escalate into larger, more expensive problems if not addressed promptly.
## Why Chip Repair Matters
* **Prevents Crack Spread**: Small chips in your windshield can quickly turn into long, unsightly cracks that obscure your view and compromise the structural integrity of your windshield.
* **Safety First**: A damaged windshield, even if just a small chip, can reduce the effectiveness of airbags and increase the risk of ejection in the event of an accident.
* **Cost-Effective**: Repairing a chip is significantly less expensive than replacing an entire windshield. Early repair can save you money in the long run.
## The Process of Chip Repair
Repairing a windshield chip involves cleaning the area around the chip, applying a special resin to fill the chip, and then using ultraviolet light to harden the resin. This process can usually be completed within 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the size and location of the chip.
