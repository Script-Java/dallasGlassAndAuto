      # Introduction to Windshield Safety
      Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that often goes overlooked until it's too late. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle and pose a significant threat to your safety on the road.

      ## Why Windshield Safety Matters
      * **Prevents Injuries**: A cracked or shattered windshield can lead to severe injuries from shards of glass and compromised airbag deployment.
      * **Maintains Vehicle Integrity**: The windshield is a crucial component of a vehicle's structure, providing up to 60% of the roof's crush resistance in a rollover accident.
      * **Ensures Unobstructed View**: A clear, undamaged windshield is essential for a driver's visibility, reducing the risk of accidents caused by obstructed views.

      ### Signs You Need a Windshield Repair
      * Chips or cracks that are spreading
      * Damage that obstructs your view
      * Leaks or water spots under the windshield
