# Introduction to Windshield Chip Repair
Windshield chips can be a nuisance, but they can also be a serious safety hazard if left unrepaired. At Dallas Glass & Auto, we understand the importance of timely windshield chip repair to ensure your safety on the road.
## Why Chip Repair is Crucial
* Prevents further damage: A small chip can spread and become a large crack, requiring a full windshield replacement.
* Saves money: Repairing a chip is significantly cheaper than replacing the entire windshield.
* Ensures safety: A damaged windshield can obstruct your view, increasing the risk of accidents.
## The Chip Repair Process
The chip repair process involves cleaning the chip, applying a special resin, and sealing the area with ultraviolet light. This process can be completed in under an hour and will leave your windshield looking like new.
