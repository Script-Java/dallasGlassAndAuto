# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that ensures the well-being of drivers and passengers. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle, posing significant risks in the event of an accident.

## Why Windshield Safety Matters
* A windshield provides up to 60% of the vehicle's structural strength in a rollover accident
* It helps to prevent ejection of occupants from the vehicle in the event of a crash
* A properly functioning windshield is essential for the deployment of airbags

## Causes of Windshield Damage
* Road debris such as rocks and gravel
* Extreme weather conditions like hail and ice
* Accidents involving other vehicles or stationary objects

## Benefits of Timely Repair
* Prevents further damage and costly repairs
* Ensures the safety of drivers and passengers
* Maintains the vehicle's structural integrity and appearance

## How to Identify Windshield Damage
* Inspect your windshield regularly for signs of damage such as chips, cracks, and scratches
* Look for areas where the glass is broken or compromised
* Check for water leaks or air noise coming from the windshield area
