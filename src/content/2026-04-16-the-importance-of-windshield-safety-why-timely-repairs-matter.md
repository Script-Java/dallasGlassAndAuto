      # Introduction to Windshield Safety
      Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A damaged windshield can pose significant risks to drivers and passengers, affecting not only the structural integrity of the vehicle but also visibility and overall safety on the road.

      ## Why Windshield Safety Matters
      - **Visibility**: A clear windshield is essential for driving safely. Any obstruction, such as a crack or chip, can impair the driver's vision, especially during sunny or rainy conditions.
      - **Structural Integrity**: The windshield plays a crucial role in the vehicle's structural integrity. It helps to maintain the vehicle's shape and provides a barrier against external forces in the event of an accident.
      - **Airbag Deployment**: In many vehicles, the windshield helps to support the deployment of airbags. A damaged windshield could compromise the effectiveness of airbag deployment, potentially leading to severe injuries.

      ## The Risks of Neglecting Windshield Repairs
      Neglecting windshield repairs can lead to more severe problems, including:
      - **Spreading Damage**: Small chips or cracks can spread rapidly, especially when exposed to temperature changes or further impacts, leading to more extensive and costly repairs.
      - **Shattering**: A compromised windshield is at a higher risk of shattering, which can happen spontaneously or due to minor impacts that a healthy windshield could withstand.
