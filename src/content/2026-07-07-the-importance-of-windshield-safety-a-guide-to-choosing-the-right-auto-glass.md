# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a crucial aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle, putting you and your passengers at risk of injury or worse in the event of an accident.
## Why Windshield Safety Matters
* A windshield provides up to 60% of a vehicle's structural integrity in a rollover accident
* A damaged windshield can shatter more easily, causing injuries from sharp glass fragments
* In many states, driving with a damaged windshield is illegal and can result in fines or even license suspension
## Choosing the Right Auto Glass
When selecting a replacement windshield, it's essential to choose a high-quality auto glass that meets or exceeds OEM standards. Here are some factors to consider:
* **OEM vs. Aftermarket**: While aftermarket windshields may be cheaper, they often lack the quality and durability of OEM glass
* **Glass Type**: Laminated glass is the safest and most durable type of glass, consisting of multiple layers of glass and plastic
* **Installation**: Make sure to choose a reputable and experienced glazier to ensure a proper installation
