# Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A small chip or crack in the windshield can quickly escalate into a full-blown problem, compromising the safety of drivers and passengers alike.
## The Dangers of Delayed Repair
* **Reduced Visibility**: Chips and cracks can spread rapidly, obscuring the driver's view and increasing the risk of accidents.
* **Structural Integrity**: The windshield is a crucial component of a vehicle's structural integrity, providing up to 30% of the vehicle's crush resistance in the event of a rollover. Damage to the windshield can compromise this integrity, putting occupants at risk.
* **Ejection Risk**: In the event of an accident, a weakened windshield can shatter, increasing the risk of occupant ejection.
## Benefits of Prompt Repair
* **Cost Savings**: Repairing a small chip or crack is significantly less expensive than replacing the entire windshield.

* **Peace of Mind**: Knowing that your windshield is in good condition can give you peace of mind, reducing stress and anxiety while driving.
