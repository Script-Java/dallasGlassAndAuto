      # Introduction to Windshield Safety
      Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that is often overlooked until it's too late. A damaged windshield can compromise the structural integrity of your vehicle and put you and your passengers at risk. In this article, we'll explore the importance of windshield safety and the benefits of professional repair and replacement services.

      ## Why Windshield Safety Matters
      * A windshield provides up to 30% of a vehicle's structural strength, making it a crucial component in the event of a collision or rollover.
      * A damaged windshield can obstruct your view, increasing the risk of accidents and injuries.
      * In the event of a crash, a windshield helps to keep occupants inside the vehicle, reducing the risk of ejection and fatal injuries.

      ## Benefits of Professional Repair and Replacement
