## Introduction to Windshield Safety
Windshield safety is a critical aspect of vehicle maintenance that ensures the well-being of drivers and passengers. A damaged windshield can pose significant risks, including decreased visibility, structural compromise, and increased chance of injury in the event of an accident.
## Why Windshield Safety Matters
* **Prevents Injury**: A windshield in good condition acts as a barrier, protecting occupants from debris and providing structural support in case of a rollover.
* **Maintains Visibility**: Clear, undamaged windshields are essential for unobstructed views, reducing the risk of accidents caused by impaired vision.
* **Supports Vehicle Structure**: The windshield is a vital component of a vehicle's cabin, contributing to its overall strength and stability.
## Signs Your Windshield Needs Repair
If you notice any of the following, it's crucial to address the issue promptly:
- Chips or cracks that obstruct your view
- Glass that is discolored or hazy
- Water leaks around the windshield edges
